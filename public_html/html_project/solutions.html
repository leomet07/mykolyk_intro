Solutions to Climate Change

Drive less. Buy less useless crap.

(but it's not that simple.)

Systemic issues of first-world countries need to be addressed.

The top economic powers, developed countries, emit hundreds of times more greenhouse gases then less developed counntries.

We buy a boat-load of oil.

Country % of imported crude oil
China 22.3%
USA 13.5%
India 10.4%
South Korea 6.5%
Japan 6.1%

Everyone, but especially (us) Americans, needs to push for more walkable environments and for our societies to be less car dependent. Our cities must stop being financially and environmentally unsustainable suburban wastelands.

We need to develop more sustainable and effecient national infrastructure (more rails, less highways).

Major industries must switch their production to be less wasteful and energy effecient.

Electrical grids must switch to zero carbon energy sources, such as wind, solor, or nuclear.

But the best thing YOU can do is get educated.

Don't just trust me, learn the science for yourself. Read through some of my linked sources at the bottom of pages.

There is personality responsiblity involved as well, not JUST corporate. We need to be less wasteful, buy less, and waste less.

Remember, your voice is loud, but so is your vote and where you spend your money.
