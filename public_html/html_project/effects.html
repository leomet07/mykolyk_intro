Effects of Climate Change

Grave damage.

Each aspect of our lives will be affected.

Here are some predicted effects.

Temp. change and the conditions that it took to get there. Work Home Food Weather Economy Health
+0°C Not affected. Expensive utilities. Occasional shortages. Current weather with some occasional irregularities. Constantly, growing economy. Some communities affected by poor air quality and by other pollutants.
+1°C Farmers and other agricultural employees struggle, urgent science needed. Even more expensive utilities, as fossil fuels deplete and become more scarce. Major issues with food chains, ecosystems, and the food industry. Common irregularities and change of biomes due to Hadley cell shift. Economy on the brink of failure. Major communities affected by pollution, relocation required.
+2°C Labor issues in all industries. Very expensive and/or unavailable utilities. Constant shortages, no food security. Doomsday weather. Failing economy, no stable currency. Everyone facing the adverse health effects of terrible air quality and terrible weather.
